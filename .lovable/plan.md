
# Yoga Teacher Portfolio Landing Page

A clean, fresh single-page website showcasing you as a yoga teacher, using your brand colors **#a46b7b** (dusty rose) and **#e1585a** (coral red) as accents against a light, airy background.

## Design Style
- Minimal, clean layout with generous whitespace
- Soft, organic feel — rounded corners, gentle shadows
- Elegant serif font for headings, clean sans-serif for body text
- Accent details using your brand colors: subtle gradient dividers, colored buttons, and decorative elements
- Smooth scroll navigation

## Sections (top to bottom)

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Fixed top bar with your name/logo on the left
- Smooth-scroll links: About, Classes, Testimonials, Contact
- Clean and minimal, becomes sticky on scroll

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-width section with a soft gradient or light background
- Large headline (e.g., "Find Your Balance") with a short tagline
- Placeholder area for your photo
- A call-to-action button ("Book a Class" or "Get in Touch") in your coral red accent color

### 3. About Me
- Split layout: photo placeholder on one side, text on the other
- Brief bio about your yoga journey, teaching philosophy, and credentials
- Subtle decorative accent line in dusty rose

### 4. Classes / Services
- Card-based grid (3 cards) showcasing different class types
- Each card: icon, class name, short description, duration/level info
- Cards use soft shadows with accent color hover effects

### 5. Testimonials
- Carousel or stacked cards with student quotes
- Name and a small avatar placeholder for each testimonial
- Subtle background color using a light tint of your brand colors

### 6. Contact Section
- Simple static contact section with your email, location, and social media links
- A decorative CTA encouraging visitors to reach out
- No backend — just displayed contact info and links

### 7. Footer
- Minimal footer with copyright, social media icon links
- Accent color subtle border or background
